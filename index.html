About Me

My name is Edgar Herrera. I am 23 years old (born December 13, 2001). I really like the color blue, I enjoy football, and I am studying a Bachelor's degree in Business Information Technology.

Advantages of knowing English

  • Access to a larger pool of information and learning resources.
  • Better job and academic opportunities internationally.
  • Improved communication when traveling and networking.
  • Enhanced cognitive skills and confidence in global environments.

Disadvantages of not knowing English

  • Limited access to many international job and study options.
  • Difficulty using many online resources and documentation.
  • Potential communication barriers when traveling or collaborating globally.
  • Fewer networking and growth opportunities with international communities.
